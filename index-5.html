DealRadical

Company Background

DealRadical Limited is an early stage startup founded by a team of experienced entrepreneurs and techies lead by Delight Aniche. We began operation in 2021 with MyEveryDeal catering for B2C customer and the company became incorporated in July the same year. The company enrolled in the Founder Insititute Acceleration program to strengthen further, our ability to deliver on the problem we want to solve for the native informal commerce ecosystem. Our mission is to make commerce stress-free using technology.

MyEveryDeal is one of the fastest growing ecommerce platforms in Nigeria and currently operates in over 40 locations across 31 states in Nigeria and 3 regions in Ghana. Zigani our logistics arm began has begun teething operation in selected cities around the country with more to come in the first quarter of 2023. Our field research and marketing team has been intensively researching the market and laying the foundation for the roll out of our other products.

Our Services

  • MyEveryDeal
  • Zigaya
  • Supbuyers

MyEveryDeal

A B2C ecommerce platform catering to hard-to-get everyday utility products and using a network of last mile logistics partners to take out presence closer to our customers thereby making ecommerce available in underserved locations at affordable cost. We focus on and give visibility to emerging utility products that are not stocked by traditional merchants and this gives us a competitive advantage.

Zigaya

A regulated B2B Ecommerce Marketplace that platforms and connects merchants to the right suppliers in various distant wholesale markets without the stress of physically travelling to the market. Major dealers and manufacturers are very busy attending to foot traffic customers in front of them that they hardly find the time to attend to remote customers at the comfort of their shop or homes while the foot traffic customers had to lockup business for days to travel for restocking. Our solution leverages digital technology, hustlers and trust protocols to bring a new customer experience to restocking for remote retailers.

Supbuyers

A Transaction Support Infrastructure Services that facilitates reliable remote transaction on behalf of the physically absent buyer by carrying out sourcing and filtering verification, validation, collation, handling, documentation, packaging, onwarding, etc. using a web of reliable agents with narrow but deep specialization in their field. Leveraging the power of social media, we aggregate and map merchants and knowledges in the unstructured informal markets to create an Online Entrepreneurs Support Community Network aimed at inspiring, training and supporting aspiring entrepreneur.

Technologies

We are leveraging different aspects of ICT like AI, machine learning, GPS tracking secureAPIs etc. to drive our innovative products.

Markets

Our service will enable service consumers, retailers and bulk buyers access varieties of service providers, suppliers and products and purchase stock from the convenience an d have it safely delivered to them. Therefore our core target market are manufacturers, major distributors, wholesalers, retailers and bulk buyers in the unstructured informal markets. The Nigerian B2B ecommerce market size according to Busisnessday is $100bn and growth at a rate of 43% per year. We will employ a freemium and service charge revenue model to grow fast and dominate the market.

Distribution

Our service will live with our customers hence we will use mobile app to put our solution in their pocket, our field teams resident in the markets and doing the heavy liftings for the customer and importantly empowering market hustlers to make their daily living by using our solution. We therefore will be using direct salesforce mainly to distribute our solution.
